Blueberry Muffins

Description

These basic muffins can be made as-is, or you can add one of several different ingredients for a variety of muffins. All options are delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
